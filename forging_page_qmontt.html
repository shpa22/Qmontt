Forging

Certified Forging Solutions

Closed-die hot steel forgings for European OEMs and industrial buyers

Qmontt AB supplies premium forging solutions to customers across Sweden and Europe through a trusted network of qualified global manufacturing partners. Our focus is on dependable forged components that deliver high strength, structural integrity, and long service life in demanding industrial applications.

We support customers from inquiry and process selection through tooling, sample validation, serial production, machining, and delivery coordination. The result is a sourcing model built around technical confidence, transparency, and practical execution.

Process Capabilities

  • Closed-die hot forging for repeatable production of robust, high-strength steel components.
  • Tooling and die development aligned with part geometry, production volumes, and cost targets.
  • Heat treatment support to achieve the mechanical properties required for demanding applications.
  • Machining and finishing for semi-finished or assembly-ready deliveries.
  • Quality documentation and traceability to support customer approval and production confidence.

Why Forging

Forging improves grain flow and internal strength, making it one of the most reliable manufacturing methods for safety-critical and high-load components. Compared with alternative routes, forged parts often provide better fatigue resistance, impact strength, and structural consistency.

Higher Strength

Improved grain structure and compact material flow for demanding mechanical loads.

Dimensional Consistency

Stable repeatability across production batches with controlled process parameters.

Reliable Performance

Excellent fatigue and impact resistance for long-life industrial components.

Production Scalability

Well suited for serial production programs once tooling and validation are completed.

Applications We Support

  • Automotive: drivetrain parts, suspension components, brackets, hubs, yokes, and structural parts.
  • Agricultural machinery: durable load-bearing parts for field equipment and mechanical systems.
  • Industrial equipment: forged parts for machines operating under repeated stress and harsh conditions.
  • Energy and infrastructure: components where reliability, consistency, and long-term durability are essential.
