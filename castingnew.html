Casting

Complete Casting Solutions

Investment, Sand, CI/SGI & Steel castings - fully machined

Qmontt AB supplies end-to-end casting solutions to European OEMs and industrial buyers through a vetted network of certified manufacturers. Our partners cover Investment Casting, Sand Casting, and Cast Iron (CI/SGI) and Steel components, with in-house machining for assembly-ready deliveries.

Materials & Processes

  • Investment Casting: Near-net shapes, complex geometries, thin walls, excellent surface finish, with a wide alloy range including stainless, aluminium, bronze, and high-performance grades.
  • Sand Casting: Steel, SG iron, and grey iron with shell-mould, no-bake, and centrifugal options for small to very large parts.
  • Cast Iron (CI) & Ductile Iron (SGI): Green sand, no-bake, cold-box, and shell-core processes for consistent quality across batch sizes.
  • Steel Castings: Capacity for precision steel components beyond iron grades, supported by machining and quality assurance.

Process Range & Capacity

  • Investment casting: approximately 10 g to 120 kg per piece.
  • Sand casting: approximately 50 g to 3000 kg per piece.
  • CI/SGI casting: approximately 0.4 kg to 400 kg per piece.
  • Scalable output: suitable for short runs, medium volumes, and repeat industrial supply.
  • Automated moulding & CNC machining: supported by automated lines and precision machining capabilities.

Machining & Finishing

Deliveries can be raw, semi-machined, or fully machined, leveraging integrated blasting, CNC, VMC, HMC, gear cutting, broaching, and grinding. This supports assembly-ready castings with dimensional reports and optional surface finishing such as powder coating.

Industries & Applications

  • Automotive: Suspension, axle, brake components, housings, brackets, and bushings.
  • Industrial Machinery & Pumps/Valves: Bodies, bonnets, housings, and mechanical sub-assemblies.
  • Waterworks: Durable components for water and sewage network installations.
  • Electrical Infrastructure: Precision accessories for medium- and high-voltage applications.
  • Agriculture & Off-Highway: OEM and aftermarket components for machinery and heavy equipment.
  • Railways & Transmission: CI/SGI components for drivetrain and structural needs.

Quality & Sustainability

  • Quality assurance: Rigorous controls from melt to machining, backed by documented inspections and traceability.
  • Repeatability: Automated moulding and precision machining to support dimensional stability at volume.
  • Sustainability: Resource optimization, scrap recycling, and environmental measures aligned with current decarbonization expectations.
