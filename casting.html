Complete Casting Solutions

Investment, Sand, CI/SGI & Steel castings - fully machined

Qmontt AB supplies end-to-end casting solutions to European OEMs and industrial buyers through a vetted network of certified manufacturers in India and Latin America. Our partners cover Investment Casting, Sand Casting, and Cast Iron (CI/SGI) and Steel components, with in-house machining for assembly-ready deliveries.

Materials & Processes

  • Investment Casting - Near net shapes, complex geometries, thin walls, excellent surface finish; wide alloy range including stainless, aluminium, bronze & high-performance grades.
  • Sand Casting - steel, SG (ductile) iron, grey iron; shell-mould, no-bake and centrifugal options for small to very large parts.
  • Cast Iron (CI) & Ductile Iron (SGI) - Green sand, no-bake, cold-box and shell-core processes for consistent quality across batch sizes.
  • Steel Castings - Capacity for precision steel components beyond iron grades, with machining and QA support.

Process Range & Scalable Casting Capacities

Production Support

Automated moulding & CNC machining through automated lines.

Machining & Finishing - One-stop Solution

Deliveries can be raw, semi-machined, or fully machined, leveraging integrated blasting, CNC/VMC/HMC, gear cutting, broaching and grinding, so you receive assembly-ready castings with dimensional reports. Parts can also be delivered powder painted.

Industries & Applications We Serve

  • Automotive - Suspension/axle/brake components, housings, brackets, bushings.
  • Industrial Machinery & Pumps/Valves - bodies, bonnets, housings, mechanical sub-assemblies.
  • Waterworks (Acueducto) - durable parts for installation of water & sewage networks.
  • Electrical Infrastructure - precision accessories for medium- and high-voltage.
  • Agriculture & Off-Highway - OEM & aftermarket components for seeding/ploughing and heavy equipment.
  • Railways & Transmission - CI/SGI components for drivetrain and structural needs.

Quality & Sustainability

  • Proven QA systems - partners operate with rigorous quality controls from melt to machining, backed by documented inspections and traceability.
  • Automated moulding and precision machining to ensure repeatability and dimensional stability at volume.
  • Environmental commitments - scrap recycling, resource optimization, and carbon-footprint measurement aligning with decarbonization expectations.
