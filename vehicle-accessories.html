Portable Refrigerators & Cooler Box Solutions

Cold-storage technology for vehicles, fleets, emergency response, and mobility applications

Qmontt offers a full range of portable refrigerators, freezers, and cooler boxes through a leading Indian manufacturer with over 20 years of automotive excellence and deep expertise in mobile cold-storage technology. These products are engineered for cars, trucks, buses, caravans, defence vehicles, and medical/vaccine logistics.

Product Range: Portable Refrigerators & Cooler Boxes

1. Portable Vehicle Refrigerators (5L - 12L+)

Compact, plug-and-play refrigerators designed for passenger cars, commercial fleets, and off-road vehicles.

Features:

  • Cooling & warming modes (up to 60°C heating depending on model)
  • DC (12V) and AC compatibility for home or in-vehicle use
  • Energy-efficient thermoelectric or compressor-based cooling
  • Compact sizes such as 5L PortaChill models ideal for cars and small cabins

Ideal For: drivers needing beverages/food on long routes, ride-share & taxi fleets, family travel, mining & construction vehicles, and defence and paramilitary vehicles for field use.

2. Portable Freezer Units

Heavy-duty cold-storage solutions built for commercial transport, logistics, medical applications, and refrigerated fleet support.

Features:

  • Deep-cooling for frozen goods
  • Rugged design for long-haul trucking
  • Suitable for perishable food, medicines, biological samples, and temperature-sensitive cargo

These units are especially suitable for sectors requiring uninterrupted cold chain reliability.

3. Vaccine Carriers & Medical Cooler Boxes

Engineered for healthcare logistics, emergency response teams, field clinics, and humanitarian operations.

Features:

  • Stable temperature maintenance for vaccines & medical supplies
  • Portable, lightweight construction
  • Preferred by vaccine transporters and disaster-response units

These medical carriers are widely used across India and emerging markets for temperature-sensitive healthcare distribution.

Commercial fleets & trucking
Buses, caravans & mobile use
Medical & vaccine logistics
