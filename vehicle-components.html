Advanced Vehicle Components

Innovative, reliable, and industry-proven automotive electronics & comfort solutions

Qmontt AB partners with a leading Indian manufacturer of automotive vehicle components and accessories, a company supplying major OEMs and known for strong R&D, indigenous manufacturing, and cutting-edge innovation. Their solutions are widely used in cars, trucks, buses, caravans, LCVs, and special military/defence vehicles.

Our Vehicle Components Portfolio

1. USB Chargers & Power Modules

A complete range of in-vehicle charging and power accessories designed for reliability and safety.

  • 12V power sockets
  • USB charging sockets (single/dual/multi-port)
  • DC-to-AC power inverters
  • DC-to-DC power converters

These systems support both passenger and commercial vehicles, ensuring drivers and passengers stay powered on the road.

2. Vehicle Surveillance & Safety Systems

Industry-recognized safety products engineered for commercial fleets, buses, logistics, mining vehicles, and defence applications.

  • Rear-view & reverse camera systems
  • Parking sensors
  • CCTV surveillance systems
  • Mobile DVR (MDVR) systems
  • Multi-camera monitoring kits

These solutions are trusted for driver safety, cargo visibility, and fleet monitoring, and are used by multiple OEMs.

3. In-Vehicle Displays & Infotainment Systems

High-performance screens and entertainment systems designed to enhance user experience.

  • LCD monitors
  • IRVM-integrated displays
  • DVD multimedia players
  • Infotainment modules for buses & premium vehicles

These systems are engineered for navigation, entertainment, and fleet communication requirements.

4. Portable Refrigerators & Cooling Systems

One of the company’s strongest product lines, widely used in both consumer and professional applications.

  • Portable vehicle refrigerators
  • Freezers
  • EV-compatible fridges
  • Vaccine carriers
  • Multi-purpose cold storage units

These products support automotive, medical, logistics, and hospitality sectors.
