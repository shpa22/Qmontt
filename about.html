About Qmontt AB

Finding a solution is our ultimate goal. Qmontt AB is a Sweden-based consultancy and sourcing partner supporting manufacturers and customers through structured, solution-driven industrial collaboration.

Who We Are

Qmontt AB is a Sweden-based consultancy and sourcing partner dedicated to helping international manufacturers succeed in Europe. We specialize in connecting innovative products and reliable industrial components with the right customers, distributors, and OEMs across the Nordic and European markets.

Our approach is practical and solution-oriented. We support customers and manufacturing partners with clear communication, technical understanding, and structured coordination from concept to delivery.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to simplify global trade, Qmontt was created to make market entry seamless for high-potential companies. Our roots span across diverse industries — from advanced vehicle components and accessories to precision castings and forgings — giving us the expertise to deliver solutions that meet European standards and customer expectations.
